Art Lab

Art-Lab is a web app for a 3D printing company. The site will serve as a place where 3D artists could bid and get 3D modeling jobs. The users needed to have a way to view current projects, past projects and new potential projects. They would also be able to perform all the neccesary transactions to bill and receive compensation.

The work required for this project included creating the Ui design.
